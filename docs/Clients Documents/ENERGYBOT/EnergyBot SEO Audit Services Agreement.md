Contractor will perform a **competitive on-page and template-level SEO audit** for Client. The scope of work includes:

* Comparison against client-provided competitors
* Review of on-page content, page templates, rendering, and dynamic components

**Deliverables:**

* One written report
* One recorded Loom video walkthrough

**Explicit Exclusions:**

* No implementation
