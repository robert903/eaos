Google Ads Account

### **Creating Your Google Ads Account**

To begin, you will need to create a Google Ads account for your business.

**1. Get Started**Visit the Google Ads homepage at ads.google.com and click "Start Now." You will be prompted to sign in with an existing Google account or create a new one. It is recommended to use a business Google account for this purpose.

**2. "Create account only"**Google will guide you through creating a campaign. It's best to skip this for now by selecting the "create account only" option.

![](data:image/png;base64...)

**3. Business Information**You will then need to provide your business information, including your company name and website.

**4. Billing Information**Finally, you will be asked to set up your billing information.

###

### **Linking Your Account to Our Manager Account (MCC)**

Once your account is set up, you will need to provide us with your Google Ads Customer ID.

**1. Locate Your Customer ID**You can find your 10-digit Customer ID in the top right corner of your Google Ads account, near your profile icon.

**2. Send Us Your Customer ID**Please send your Customer ID to us. Once we have it, we will send a request from our MCC account to link to yours.

**3. Approve the Linking Request**You will receive a notification in your Google Ads account to approve our request. To do this:

* Click the **Admin** icon in the bottom left of the main menu.
* Select **Access and Security**.
* Go to the **Managers** tab.
* You will see a pending link request from our agency. Click **Accept** to approve the request.

By following these steps, you will grant our agency the necessary administrative access to manage your Google Ads campaigns efficiently through our MCC.

Linking Tiktok Ad Assets

### **Part 1: Create Your TikTok Ad Account**

First, we will create the account that will be used to run all of your advertisements.

1. **Log in to your Business Center** at: [business.tiktok.com](https://business.tiktok.com)
2. In the menu on the left, go to the **Assets** section and click on **Ad accounts**.
3. Click the blue **Add ad account** button.
4. A pop-up will appear. Select **Create new**.
5. Follow the on-screen instructions:
   * **Ad account name:** Give it a clear name, like brand.ai - Main Ad Account.
   * **Time zone:** Select your correct business time zone.
   * **Billing currency:** Select your currency.
6. Click **Confirm**.

Once the Ad Account is created, please proceed immediately to Part 2.

### **Part 2: Grant Us Access to Your New Ad Account**

Now that the Ad Account exists, you must assign it to our agency.

1. While logged in to your Business Center, go to the **Users** section on the left menu and click on **Partners**.
2. You will see our agency, **Elegant Atomics**, listed as a partner. Click on our name.
3. Click the blue **Assign new assets** button.
4. In the new window, select **Ad Accounts** from the list on the left.
5. In the middle column, **check the box** next to the Ad Account you just created (brand.ai - Main Ad Account).
6. In the right-hand column, assign us the **Admin** role. This is required for us to fully manage your campaigns.
7. Click the blue **Assign** button at the bottom to save.

### **Part 3: Create Your TikTok Pixel**

Next, we will create the Pixel, which is the bit of code used to track website visitors and conversions.

1. In your Business Center, go back to the **Assets** section on the left and click on **Events**.
2. Under the "Website Pixel" section, click the **Create Pixel** button.
3. **Name your Pixel** (e.g., brand.ai Website Pixel) and click **Next**.
4. The system will ask you about installation. You do not need to do anything here. We will handle the technical installation on your website once we have access. You can simply close this window.

Once the Pixel is created, please proceed immediately to Part 4.

### **Part 4: Grant Us Access to Your New Pixel**

Finally, you must assign the new Pixel to our agency so we can manage it.

1. Go back to the **Users** section on the left menu and click on **Partners**.
2. Click on our agency name, **Elegant Atomics**, again.
3. Click the blue **Assign new assets** button.
4. In the new window, select **Pixels** from the list on the left.
5. In the middle column, **check the box** next to the Pixel you just created (brand.ai Website Pixel).
6. In the right-hand column, assign us the **Admin** role. This allows us to manage tracking and optimize your campaigns.
7. Click the blue **Assign** button at the bottom to save.

### **Part 5: Add Your TikTok Account (@brand.ai)**

Now, connect your company's TikTok profile (@brand.ai) to the Business Center. This allows us to run "Spark Ads" that look like native posts from your account.

1. In your Business Center, go to the **Assets** section on the left and click **TikTok accounts**.

7. Tap the **scanner icon** in the top right of the QR code screen.
8. **Scan the QR code** shown on your computer screen.
9. Follow the prompts on your phone to **Authorize** the Business Center connection.
