### **Proposal: An Integrated Organic Growth Strategy for Datacoves**

**Prepared for:** Noel Gomez, Datacoves
**Prepared by:** Trevor Fox
**Date:** November 12, 2025

### **1. Understanding the Challenge: Navigating the New Era of Discovery**

From our conversation, it's clear that while past SEO efforts have yielded significant wins (like ranking for "dbt alternatives"), the digital marketing environment is undergoing a fundamental shift. The decline in site traffic you've noticed is a common challenge as potential customers increasingly turn to AI-powered tools (like ChatGPT) and community forums for discovery and answers.

The core problem is one of **awareness and attribution**. In this new era, the goal is no longer just to rank #1 on Google, but to ensure Datacoves is present and cited wherever your ideal customer is looking for solutions. We need to shift from a broad traffic-based approach to a highly targeted strategy focused on attracting high-intent buyers and becoming a trusted voice in the ecosystem.

This proposal outlines a multi-faceted organic growth strategy designed to address this challenge head-on, positioning Datacoves for sustainable growth by aligning content, targeted ads, and community engagement with your unique product-market fit.

### **2. Proposed Strategy: The Three Pillars of Growth**

I propose a monthly consulting engagement where I act as your strategic partner for organic growth. Our work will be built on three core pillars:

#### **Pillar 1: Foundational Strategy & Targeted Content**

We will translate your deep understanding of your customers into a precise and actionable content plan.

* **Product-Market Fit Deep Dive:** We will start by reviewing your Ideal Customer Profile (ICP), positioning, and messaging guide.
* **Targeted Content Roadmapping:** We will develop a content strategy that moves beyond broad keywords. Instead, we'll focus on the specific, long-tail problems your ICP faces, such as "how to orchestrate dbt and Python together" or "standardizing dbt developer environments for large teams." This ensures your content directly addresses the nuanced needs of high-value prospects.

#### **Pillar 2: Maximizing Coverage & Citations**

Our goal is to be everywhere your potential buyers are. That means dominating traditional long-tail search results while also optimizing for citations in forums and AI-generated answers.

* **Identifying Citation Opportunities:** We will actively identify and pursue opportunities to have Datacoves mentioned and recommended in key online discussions.
* **Forum & User-Generated Content (UGC) Strategy:** We will build a strategic plan for engaging on the platforms where your audience lives. This includes:
  + **Reddit & LinkedIn:** Refining your current "guerrilla marketing" tactics to strategically insert Datacoves into relevant conversations.
  + **Quora, GitHub Gists, Medium & Substack:** Creating authoritative content that answers specific technical questions, positioning Datacoves as a helpful expert.
  + **YouTube:** Exploring opportunities for video content that addresses key technical challenges.

#### **Pillar 3: Immediate ROI & Lead Activation**

While our organic strategy builds momentum, we can capture existing demand and capitalize on website interest immediately.

* **Targeted Search Ads:** I propose launching a focused Google Ads campaign targeting high-intent, bottom-of-the-funnel keywords like "dbt alternatives." This will provide an immediate source of qualified leads and valuable ROI while we build our organic presence.
* **"Warmbound" Lead Program:** Many valuable prospects visit your site, look at pricing, and leave without converting. I will help you set up a simple but effective "warmbound" program to identify companies showing high-intent signals and proactively reach out to them, bridging the gap between anonymous website traffic and qualified sales conversations.

### **3. Reporting & The Feedback Loop: Measuring What Matters**

To ensure our strategy is effective, we will move beyond vanity metrics and focus on a holistic view of performance.

* **Comprehensive Reporting:** You will receive a monthly report detailing:
  + Performance of targeted organic keywords.
  + Mentions and citations of Datacoves in LLM/AI search results.
  + Performance and ROI of the search ads campaign.
* **Understanding Attribution:** We will work to refine your "how did you hear about us?" process to better attribute inbound leads to specific channels and campaigns.
* **Regular Review & Learning:** We will hold regular strategy calls to review progress, analyze what's working, and adapt our plan. This completes the feedback loop, ensuring our efforts are constantly improving.
