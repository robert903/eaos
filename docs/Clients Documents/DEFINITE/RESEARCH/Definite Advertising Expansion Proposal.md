Our current Google Ads campaign, targeting general startup analytics keywords, is successfully spending its $30/day budget to attract relevant prospects. To accelerate growth, we recommend a strategic expansion into three new campaign areas. These campaigns will target users searching for alternatives to your competitors, specific competitor pricing information, and analytics solutions that integrate with their existing software stack.

To support this expansion, we propose increasing the daily Google Ads budget by $90, bringing the total to **$120**.

This will allocate a $30/day budget to each of the three new campaigns, mirroring the budget of our current successful campaign.

### **Current Campaign Overview**

* **Daily Budget:** $30
* **Keyword Focus:** General terms like "[startup data analytics]" and "[analytics tools for startups]".
* **Objective:** Attract startups actively seeking analytics and data visualization solutions.
* **Strategy:** Drive high-intent traffic for direct conversions or identification for targeted email outreach.

This campaign is effectively reaching prospects in the initial awareness and consideration stages. The proposed expansion is designed to capture users further down the funnel who are actively evaluating and comparing solutions.

### **Proposed Campaign Expansion**

We have identified a significant opportunity to capture more high-intent traffic by targeting keywords that signal a user is actively looking to make a purchase decision. We propose launching three new, highly-targeted search campaigns:

**Campaign 2: Competitor Alternatives**

* **Objective:** Capture market share from competitors by targeting users actively seeking a replacement. These prospects are already problem-aware and solution-aware, making them highly qualified leads.
* **Sample Keywords:** "metabase alternative", "looker studio alternative", "domo alternative", "superset alternative", "panoply alternative", "mozart analytics alternative", "cheaper alternative to snowflake", "open source bigquery alternative".

**Campaign 3: Competitor Pricing**

* **Objective:** Attract prospects who are in the final stages of their decision-making process. Searches for pricing indicate a strong buying signal. This allows us to position Definite.app as a cost-effective and transparent solution.
* **Sample Keywords:** "metabase pricing", "looker studio cost", "domo pricing", "superset cost", "panoply cost", "mozart analytics cost".

**Campaign 4: Integration & Platform-Specific Analytics**

* **Objective:** Target users who are looking for analytics solutions that work with the tools they already use. This demonstrates Definite.app's value within their existing ecosystem and targets a direct pain point.
* **Sample Keywords:** "hubspot analytics", "salesforce analytics", "pipedrive analytics", "stripe analytics", "quickbooks analytics", "zendesk analytics".

### **Expected Outcomes**

This strategic expansion is designed to:

* **Increase High-Intent Traffic:** By targeting long-tail and competitor keywords, we will attract users who are much closer to a purchasing decision.
* **Generate More Qualified Leads:** The highly specific nature of the new campaigns will result in traffic that is more likely to convert or be a strong candidate for our sales outreach.
* **Improve Market Intelligence:** We will gain valuable insights into which competitor and platform integrations are generating the most interest, allowing for further strategic refinement.
* **Accelerate Growth:** Capturing this qualified traffic will directly contribute to your goals of increasing conversions and building a robust pipeline of prospects.
