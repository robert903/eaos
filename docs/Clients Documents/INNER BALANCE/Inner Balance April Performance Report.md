# **Inner Balance Performance Report: Last 28 Days**

This report analyzes Inner Balance's SEO performance over the last 28 days (April 2025), **focusing on two key segments: branded queries and blog post search queries**. It highlights significant events, quantifies improvements in impressions and clicks, and provides recommendations to sustain and enhance growth.

![](data:image/png;base64...)

* Total Impressions: **Increased from 11,000 to 27,100 (+146%)** over the last 28 days.
* Total Clicks: **Grew from 675 to 1,790 (+165%)** over the last 28 days.

These gains reflect the positive impact of SEO efforts, despite challenges with URL changes and indexing delays we’ve experienced.

## **Section 1: Key Events Timeline and Impact**

### **Timeline of Key Events**

* **April 10, 2025**: Added /p prefix to Journal blog post URLs, changing from /learn/\* to /p/learn/\*. This caused all blog post URLs to 404 temporarily, disrupting momentum as Google needed to recrawl and reindex the pages.
* **April 15, 2025**: Implemented brand optimizations.
* **April 16, 2025**: Submitted sitemap, commencing the indexing of blog content.

### **Explanation of Impact**

The URL prefix change on April 10 disrupted ongoing momentum, as all /learn/\* URLs temporarily 404ed, requiring Google to recrawl and reindex the new /p/learn/\* structure. This reset progress for blog posts, leading to fluctuations in traffic and impressions. The brand optimizations on **April 15** contributed to some growth in branded query performance, particularly for *"Oestra"* and *"Inner Balance"* The sitemap submission on **April 16** facilitated better indexing of blog content, though slow indexing behavior persisted due to the earlier URL changes.

## **Section 3 - Blog Post Performance**

Blog posts exhibited a positive overall trend, with increased traffic and impressions over the last three months, driven by a higher volume of content and SEO improvements. However, the URL changes on April 10 introduced inconsistencies that tempered these gains.

![](data:image/png;base64...)
(The graph above illustrates positive growth in blog search performance over the last 3 months)

#### **URL Change Impact**

The transition from /learn/\* to /p/learn/\* on April 10 resulted in temporary 404 errors, interrupting momentum and causing fluctuations in blog post performance due to slow indexing by Google. A 28-day comparison highlights the timeline of these changes: following the sitemap submission, which included the updated /p/learn/\* URLs, traffic to the new URLs initially increased. However, this uptick was short-lived, as total impressions to the new URLs subsequently declined, reflecting ongoing indexing challenges.

**![](data:image/png;base64...)**(The graph above illustrates the blog url change impact. Track the new /p/learn/\* urls using the dotted purple lines)

#### **Specific URL Analysis**

The blog post "Before and After Hormone Replacement Therapy: 6 Women’s Stories" initially contributed significantly to the overall impressions for /p/learn/\* URLs. However, its rankings subsequently declined, likely due to the presence of excessively long anchor text (e.g., 12-word links [flagged in Slack](https://innerbalancegroup.slack.com/archives/C08E8FDAD0T/p1745858592503569)). Such lengthy anchor text can signal low-quality content to Google, negatively impacting search performance.

## **Key Takeaways and Next Steps**

To build on the progress made in April and address challenges, we recommend the following actions:

* **Stabilize URLs**: Avoid further URL changes to maintain indexing stability and prevent disruptions.
* **Optimize Anchor Text**: Review and shorten anchor text links routinely (e.g., reduce 12-word links to 3 or 4 words) to eliminate spammy signals and improve rankings.
* **Monitor Key URLs**: Closely track the "Bioidentical Hormone Replacement Therapy" post for recovery and investigate additional factors affecting its performance.
* **Focus on Branded Queries**: Continue optimizing for "Inner Balance," "Oestra," and "Dr. Sara Daccarett" to sustain and accelerate growth in clicks and impressions.
* **Enhance Indexing**: Leverage the sitemap submission to ensure all blog content is indexed promptly, and monitor Google Search Console for indexing issues.

Branded queries showed strong growth, particularly for "Oestra," while blog posts faced challenges due to URL changes and indexing delays. By stabilizing URLs, optimizing anchor text, and focusing on branded queries, Inner Balance can build on this momentum to achieve sustained SEO success.
