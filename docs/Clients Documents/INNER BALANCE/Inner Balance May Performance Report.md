## **Blog Search Performance**

Blog posts showed a strong upward trend, which boosted traffic and impressions over the past three months, fueled by increased content volume and SEO enhancements. A positive trend in impression share emerged following the blog's reindexing on April 16.

![](data:image/png;base64...)

### **URL Change Impact**

On April 10, the shift from /learn/\* to /p/learn/\* caused temporary 404 errors, disrupting momentum and leading to inconsistent blog post performance due to delayed Google indexing. A 28-day analysis indicates consistent progress after Google indexed the /p/learn/\* URLs, with impression share doubling, though average search position continues to trail.

**![](data:image/png;base64...)**(The graph above illustrates the blog url change impact. Track the new /p/learn/\* urls using the dotted purple lines)

### **Specific URL Analysis**

The blog post "Before and After Hormone Replacement Therapy: 6 Women’s Stories" initially boosted impressions for /p/learn/\* URLs but later experienced a ranking decline, likely due to excessively long anchor text (e.g., 12-word links [noted in Slack](https://innerbalancegroup.slack.com/archives/C08E8FDAD0T/p1745858592503569)), which Google may interpret as low-quality content, impacting search performance. Impressions for this blog post dropped by over 50%, while average position and clicks remained mostly stable over the last 28 days.
