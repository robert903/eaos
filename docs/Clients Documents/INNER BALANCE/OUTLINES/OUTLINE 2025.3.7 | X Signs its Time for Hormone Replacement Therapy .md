### X Signs its Time for Hormone Replacement Therapy

### Strategy

This article is a bridge between informational queries about conditions and symptoms and HRT-centric content.

This foundational post’s purpose is not to sell directly. It has two primary purposes:

1. Provide broad information about the factors that contribute to *when* a woman might consider HRT (age, specific symptoms)
2. Create a link between menopause symptoms and the benefits of HRT

The only marginally salesy aspect of this article is in the conclusion section that addresses the question of whether the reader should take HRT.

**Few articles address this question directly:** The articles that rank for this topic are general overviews of HRT.

**To differentiate** this article from the dozens of other articles on the same topic, *we’re going to provide the most contextual medical conditions and stages of life that suggest the need for HRT.*

**Tone:** educational, unassuming, a trusted guide

**Word count:** 1000-1500
