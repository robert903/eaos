360e is currently missing out on high-intent traffic that competitors are intercepting. This engagement is designed to capture that "left on the table" revenue by launching precise, high-efficiency Google Ads campaigns.

# Scope of Work

In this 3-month engagement, we will build, launch, and optimize a customer acquisition engine designed to lower your Cost Per Acquisition (CPA) through high ad relevance.

## Phase 1: Targeting (Month 1)

We start by identifying the specific, problem-aware searches that competitors are ignoring.

* **Deep-Dive Research:** Identify keywords that signal a disconnect between the field and the office, such as "electrical T&M billing" or "scheduling software for electricians". These terms attract serious buyers looking for immediate solutions, bypassing the expensive general research phase.
* **Brand Defense:** Launch a low-cost campaign to ensure 360e appears at the top of the results when users search for your name, preventing competitors from poaching your referrals.
* **Granular Ad Creation:** Write specific ad copy for each niche keyword group to ensure 9/10 or 10/10 Quality Scores.
* **Tracking Setup:** Configure conversion tracking to ensure every lead is measured accurately.

## Phase 2: Data Analysis & Refinement (Month 2)

Once traffic is flowing, we shift to optimization to lower costs and improve lead quality.

* **Search Term Analysis:** Review exactly what users are typing to trigger our ads.
* **Negative Keyword Management:** Aggressively filter out waste (e.g., users looking for "free templates" or "jobs") to protect the budget.
* **Quality Score Optimization:** Adjust ad copy and landing page relevance to push Quality Scores up and actual CPCs down.

## Phase 3: Continuous Optimization & Scale (Month 3)

With a baseline established, we focus on efficiency and expansion.

* **Bid Strategy Adjustment:** Move from manual control to data-backed bidding strategies where appropriate.
* **Audience Layering:** Implement remarketing lists and "In-Market" audiences to refine targeting further.
* **Performance Reporting:** A full breakdown of CPA, conversion rates, and strategic recommendations for the next stage of growth.

# Recommended Ad Spend & Traffic Projections

For the Phase 1 launch, I recommend a starting media budget of **$300/month** (paid directly to Google). Based on current bid estimates for these niche keywords, we project this will generate **50–75 highly qualified clicks per month**.

While this volume is lower than social media metrics, the intent is significantly higher. Unlike Facebook users who are scrolling, these are users actively searching for specific solutions to their pain points (e.g., "inventory tracking"). Consequently, these 50–75 visitors have a much higher probability of converting into leads than broad traffic.
