### **🚨 Critical Issues 🚨**

**a. Broken Links (404 Errors) & Images 🔗**

* **⚠️ Issue:** Several pages and images on the site are broken or missing. This frustrates users and wastes search engine crawl budget.
  + **Pages:** /job-scheduling-software/, /reporting/, and others are leading to 404 errors.
  + **Images:** Images on the homepage, success stories, and several blog posts are not loading.
* **🛠️ Fix:**
  + Redirect all broken URLs to the most relevant live page using 301 redirects.
  + Replace all broken images to improve the site's visual presentation.

**b. Duplicate Pages 📄📄**

* **⚠️ Issue:** The blog post "Deductions That Save Electricians Major Cash" exists on two separate URLs. This confuses search engines and splits the page's ranking power.
* **🛠️ Fix:**
  + Select one URL as the main version.
  + Apply a 301 redirect from the duplicate page to the chosen primary page.

### **📈 On-Page SEO Opportunities 📈**

**a. Meta Descriptions 📝**

* **⚠️ Issue:** A large number of pages, especially blog posts, are missing meta descriptions. Additionally, the /tour/ and /free-trial/ pages use the same description. This leads to less effective search results.
* **🛠️ Fix:**
  + Write unique, engaging meta descriptions for all pages that are missing them.
  + Give the /tour/ and /free-trial/ pages distinct descriptions that highlight their unique purpose.

**b. Heading Tags (H1 & H2) 🔖**

* **⚠️ Issue:** Key pages like /free-trial/ and /pricing/ are missing a primary H1 heading. Many blog posts also share a generic H1 ("FROM THE FIELD") and H2 ("BLOG"), which is a missed opportunity for relevance.
* **🛠️ Fix:**
  + Ensure every page has one unique, descriptive H1 tag.
  + Update the generic headings on blog posts to be specific to the article's topic (e.g., use the article title as the H1).

**c. Short Page Titles 🏷️**

* **⚠️ Issue:** Some pages like /terms/ and /privacy-policy/ have very short, non-descriptive titles.
* **🛠️ Fix:**
  + Expand these titles to be more descriptive and include the brand name (e.g., "Privacy Policy | my360e").

### **🔗 External Linking 🔗**

**a. Excessive Outbound Links 📤**

* **⚠️ Issue:** A significant number of blog posts have over 45 external links. This can dilute your website's authority and may appear spammy to search engines if the links aren't high-quality.
* **🛠️ Fix:**
  + Review these blog posts and remove any external links that are not absolutely necessary or that point to low-quality sources.

### **🚀 Priority Action Plan 🚀**

1. **Fix Broken Links & Images:** Immediately address all 404 errors and broken images to improve user experience.
2. **Resolve Duplicate Content:** Consolidate the duplicate blog post into a single URL.
3. **Optimize On-Page Elements:** Begin by adding unique H1 tags and meta descriptions to your most important pages.
4. **Review Outbound Links:** Audit blog posts with many external links and prune unnecessary ones.
