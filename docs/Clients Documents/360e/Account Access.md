### **Linking Your Google Ads Account to Our Manager Account (MCC)**

Once your account is set up, you will need to provide us with your Google Ads Customer ID.

**1. Locate Your Customer ID**You can find your 10-digit Customer ID in the top right corner of your Google Ads account, near your profile icon.

**2. Send Us Your Customer ID**Please send your Customer ID to us. Once we have it, we will send a request from our MCC account to link to yours.

**3. Approve the Linking Request**You will receive a notification in your Google Ads account to approve our request. To do this:

* Click the **Admin** icon in the bottom left of the main menu.
* Select **Access and Security**.
* Go to the **Managers** tab.
* You will see a pending link request from our agency. Click **Accept** to approve the request.

By following these steps, you will grant our agency the necessary administrative access to manage your Google Ads campaigns efficiently through our MCC.
