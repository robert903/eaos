## **Key Takeaways**

* **Technical Issues Likely Caused Performance Slump:** The most significant finding this month is that a major technical glitch, which caused website pages to show up blank, was discovered and fixed. This issue offers a clear explanation for the sharp drop in conversions and likely contributed to the overall decline in user engagement and other key organic metrics.
* **Paid Search ROI Impacted by Website Health:** The increased investment in Google Ads did not yield the expected results, with conversions dropping to just one. This poor return on investment is not seen as a reflection of campaign quality, but rather a direct consequence of the on-site technical issues that prevented users from converting after clicking an ad.
* **Improved Search Ranking is the Top Story:** The most significant achievement this month is the dramatic improvement in average search position, indicating that SEO strategies are successfully boosting the site's authority and relevance.
