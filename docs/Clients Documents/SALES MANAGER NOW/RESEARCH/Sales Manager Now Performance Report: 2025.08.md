## **Key Takeaways**

* **#1 Problem: Missed Opportunity on Core Terms:** The primary challenge remains the failure to convert high impression counts for core commercial keywords into a correspondingly high number of clicks.
* **Paid Search Funnel Remains Broken:** The ad spend of $440.61 in August resulted in zero attributable leads, highlighting a critical failure in the post-click funnel that needs immediate attention.
* **Organic Search Remains the Value Driver:** Despite a decrease in overall impressions, organic search was the sole channel delivering qualified leads in August, justifying a concentrated effort to resolve organic CTR and traffic issues.
* **Content Visibility is Not Translating to Traffic:** While some pages are being rewarded with more visibility, the lack of corresponding click growth on many key pages represents the single largest opportunity for immediate traffic recovery.

## **Next Steps**

* **Priority #1: Immediately Capitalize on the Success of High-Performing Pages (Organic):** The most significant opportunity is to build on the success of the "/what-is-fractional-sales-management/" page. The immediate action is to further optimize the on-page elements and meta descriptions of this page to continue improving its click-through rate.
* **Priority #2: Diagnose the Site-Wide Impression Drop:** It is crucial to understand the root cause of the 10.46% decline in overall visibility. An in-depth analysis should be conducted to determine which sections of the site, keyword categories, or page types lost the most impressions. This will help to inform the broader content and SEO strategy.
* **Priority #3: Build a Content Moat Around "Fractional Sales Manager":** Given the high impression volume for "fractional sales manager," there is a clear signal from Google that the site has authority on this topic. The next step is to leverage this by creating targeted blog posts, downloadable resources, and case studies around this theme to capture a wider audience and provide more entry points to the site for this high-value search term.
