## **Key Takeaways**

* **Overall Performance & Channel Mix:** Sales Manager Now achieved strong search visibility gains organically (**15.9% impression increase**), complemented by effective ad engagement (**9.98% overall Google Ads CTR**). While organic clicks saw a **9.5% decrease**, leads are being generated from a diverse mix of channels, including organic search, paid search (Google CPC), and LinkedIn referrals, indicating multiple effective pathways to prospects.
* **Key Search Term & Campaign Performance:** The brand term **"sales manager now"** remains dominant organically. High-intent organic terms like **"fractional sales manager"** and **"fractional sales leader"** are showing excellent click growth. In paid search, the **"Fractional Sales Management" Google Ads campaign is the sole converter** and shows strong CTR, while campaigns like "Part Time Sales Management" also exhibit high CTRs but require conversion optimization.
* **Channel Effectiveness & Audience Engagement:** Organic search and Google Ads are demonstrably driving leads. LinkedIn, in particular, shows significant influence both as a direct referral source and as an initial touchpoint for brand discovery, especially within relevant communities (e.g., EOS). Marketing efforts are successfully reaching senior decision-makers.
* **Content & Conversion Optimization Opportunities:** While cornerstone content like the "fractional sales manager" post shows visibility, infrequent broader blog posting may limit organic engagement. Critically, high Google Ads CTRs for several campaigns without corresponding conversions point to significant opportunities for **landing page optimization** to improve the post-click experience and overall conversion rates.

## **Next Steps**

1. **Enhance Click-Through & Conversion Rates (Organic & Paid):**
   * **Organic CTR:** Continue to review and optimize meta titles and descriptions for pages with high impressions but low organic CTR, making them more compelling and benefit-driven. Analyze SERP features (e.g., AI Overviews) and adapt content.
   * **Paid Ad Conversions:** For Google Ads campaigns with high CTR but no conversions (e.g., "Part Time Sales Management," "Fractional Sales Director"), critically review and A/B test landing page content, calls-to-action, and user experience to ensure alignment with ad promises and improve conversion rates.
   * **Underperforming Ads:** Investigate and refine underperforming Google Ads campaigns (e.g., "Sales Management Consulting") by testing ad copy, keyword specificity, and bidding strategies. Consider pausing or reallocating budget from consistently low-performing ad efforts.
2. **Leverage Successful Channels & Content:**
   * **Scale Winning Ad Campaigns:** Cautiously increase budget for the converting "Fractional Sales Management" Google Ads campaign, closely monitoring Cost per Conversion. Analyze its success elements (keywords, ad copy, landing page) to apply learnings to other campaigns.
   * **Cornerstone Content (Organic):** Continuously update and refine the "fractional sales manager" post with fresh insights and optimized meta content. Identify and develop other cornerstone pieces for high-intent keywords like "fractional sales leader".
     Expand on adjacent content by either adding to existing content or publishing new content
   * **LinkedIn Strategy:** Develop a strategy to further leverage LinkedIn for brand building and lead generation, given its observed influence. This could include targeted content sharing, engagement with relevant groups, or outreach, particularly focusing on connections like the EOS community.
3. **Strategic Content Creation & Expansion:**
   * **Blog Frequency:** Increase the frequency of high-quality blog posts targeting valuable non-brand keywords to expand organic reach, drive engagement, and improve rankings for a broader set of terms.
