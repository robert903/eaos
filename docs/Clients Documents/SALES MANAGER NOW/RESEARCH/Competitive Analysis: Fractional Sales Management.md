# **Competitive Analysis: “Fractional Sales Management”**

This document analyzes the ads, landing pages, and service offerings of key players in the fractional sales management market. It breaks down how each company targets a different customer segment by competing on distinct value propositions like speed, cost, quality, or strategic depth.

## **Sales Manager Now**

### **Landing Page Service Offering:**

Sales Manager Now provides part-time, fractional sales managers specifically tailored for small and family-owned businesses. They act as the client's sales manager, focusing on coaching, process implementation, and performance management, with a clear, tiered pricing structure based on the number of salespeople being managed.

* **Service Highlights**:
  + Specializes in serving small businesses and family businesses since 2006.
  + Offers a "Part-Time Sales Management System."
  + Provides transparent, tiered pricing based on team size (e.g., 1-2 Salespeople for $3,900/month).
  + Services include hiring, performance management, process development, and team coaching.
  + Engagements are month-to-month, emphasizing a results-based relationship.
* **Prominent Focus Keyword**: Fractional Sales Manager
* **Relevance to "Fractional Sales Management":**
  The landing page is highly relevant, focusing exclusively on providing fractional and part-time sales managers with a unique and transparent pricing model. **Grade: 10/10**

## **Activated Scale** - <https://www.activatedscale.com/fractional-sales-leadership>

### **Landing Page Service Offering:**

Activated Scale is a talent marketplace that connects businesses with pre-vetted, US-based fractional sales leaders. They position themselves as a flexible and cost-effective alternative to hiring a full-time VP of Sales, offering talent on a monthly retainer.

* **Service Highlights:**
  + Provides access to vetted and experienced US-based sales talent.
  + Operates on a monthly retainer model for fractional leadership.
  + Offers flexibility and scalability, allowing companies to adjust involvement as needed.
  + Provides an objective, external perspective on sales strategy and operations.
  + Bridges the leadership gap during the search for a permanent hire.
* **Prominent Focus Keyword**: Fractional Sales Leadership
* **Relevance to "Fractional Sales Management":**
  This page is perfectly aligned, using the exact terminology and addressing all core aspects of the fractional sales leadership value proposition. **Grade: 10/10**

### **Ad Value Proposition:**

The primary value proposition is speed and a low-risk path to a full-time hire. The headline promises a hire in under 10 days, directly addressing the pain point of a long and difficult hiring process. The stat "65% Of Hires Become Full-Time" uniquely positions them as a "try-before-you-buy" talent solution.

| Hire a Fractional Sales Leader |
| --- |
| <10 Days To Hire Sales Talent — Need Sales Help Fast? Hire Vetted US SDR’s in 10 Days. 65% Of Hires Become Full-Time. |

* **Optimization**:
  + Urgency & Data: They use hard numbers ("<10 Days", "65%") to build credibility and create a sense of efficiency.
  + Keyword Mismatch: The ad mentions "Fractional Sales Leader" in the headline but "Vetted US SDR’s" in the description. This suggests they are targeting a broad audience who may use "leader" when they actually need an executor (SDR).
  + Pain Point Agitation: The line "Need Sales Help Fast?" is a direct, question-based call-to-action that resonates with a desperate searcher.
* **Focus**: Their focus is on companies that need immediate sales execution help, not necessarily high-level strategy. They appeal to hiring managers or founders who are frustrated with traditional recruiting timelines and want to fill a role (likely a senior SDR or a hands-on team lead) quickly.

## **Wing** - <https://wingassistant.com/sales-development-representative>

### **Landing Page Service Offering:**

Wing is a talent-as-a-service provider that supplies remote Sales Development Representatives (SDRs) to handle lead prospecting and outreach. Their service is designed to build the sales pipeline, not to manage the sales team or strategy.

  + Focuses on engaging prospects, growing the sales pipeline, and generating revenue through outreach.
  + Offers an optional "Sales Boost" service for lead prospecting.
  + Provides a "Wing Workspace App" to manage tasks and communication with the SDR.
* **Prominent Focus Keyword**: Sales Development Representatives (SDRs)
* **Relevance to "Fractional Sales Management":**
  This page is not relevant as it focuses on hiring individual contributors (SDRs), not the sales managers or leaders the user is searching for. **Grade: 2/10**

The value proposition is overwhelmingly low cost. They lead with aggressive, specific pricing making price their main differentiator.
