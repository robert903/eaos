## **Key Takeaways**

* **Overall Performance**: Sales Manager Now’s website saw a **5.9% increase in impressions** (from 76,800 to 81,300), indicating enhanced search visibility, but a **20% drop in clicks** (from 320 to 256) and a decline in **average position** (from 39 to 42.3) highlight challenges in engaging users and maintaining rankings.
* **Key Search Terms Performance**: The **#1 rankings** for “sales manager now” and “fractional sales manager” underscore strong brand authority, with brand queries achieving a **3.65% impression growth** (from 13,700 to 14,200). However, a **19.35% click decline** (from 37 to 31) for these terms suggests less compelling search snippets or increased competition.
* **Impact of Infrequent Posting**: The **limited frequency of blog posts** (only one in the last 3 months) restricts fresh content to attract clicks and bolster rankings for key search terms like “part-time sales manager.” **More frequent posting** can drive engagement, improve click-through rates, and strengthen visibility by targeting high-intent queries.
