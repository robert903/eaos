### **Google Ads Strategic Growth Plan for Sales Manager Now (SMN)**

This document outlines a three-phase strategic plan to enhance the performance and scale the impact of the Sales Manager Now (SMN) Google Ads account. Our current success with high-intent Exact Match keywords has validated the channel. The next steps are to methodically expand our reach, aggressively capture competitor market share, and build a defensive moat around our brand to protect our most valuable traffic. This multi-pronged approach will drive lead volume, lower client acquisition costs, and solidify SMN's position as a market leader.

### **Phase 1: Scaling Core Campaigns with Phrase Match**

**Objective:** To expand our reach beyond our current known keywords, discover new, profitable search queries, and capture a wider audience of potential clients, often at a lower Cost.

**Rationale:**Exact Match campaigns are excellent for control and proving a concept. However, they are limited by what we think users search for. Phrase Match allows us to move from a purely controlled environment to a "discovery" phase. We will capture valuable long-tail searches (e.g., "fractional sales manager for small businesses") that signal high intent but have lower search volume and competition. This provides invaluable market intelligence while simultaneously generating leads.

**Action Plan:**

1. **Campaign Structure:** We will not add Phrase Match keywords to existing ad groups. Instead, we will create new, parallel ad groups (or a separate campaign) for Phrase Match keywords.
   * Example: For an ad group named "Fractional Sales Manager - Exact," we will create a new ad group named "Fractional Sales Manager - Phrase."
   * Benefit: This isolates variables, allowing us to set different bids, allocate specific budgets, and clearly compare the performance of Exact vs. Phrase match types.
2. **Proactive Negative Keyword Implementation:** To maintain relevance and control costs, we will build a robust negative keyword list before launching. This will include terms irrelevant to SMN's services, such as:
   * **Employment-related:** jobs, career, salary, resume, hiring
   * **Informational:** free, template, examples, course, certification
   * **Irrelevant Geographies:** [names of cities/states SMN doesn't serve]
3. **Ongoing Search Term Review:** For the first 30 days, we will review the Search Terms report **daily**. Any irrelevant search query that triggers an ad will be immediately added as a negative keyword to prevent wasted ad spend.

### **Phase 2: Market Capture with Competitor Campaigns**

**Objective:** To intercept high-intent prospects at the exact moment they are evaluating SMN's direct competitors. This allows us to present SMN as a superior alternative and capture leads that would have otherwise been lost.

**Validity & Rationale:**A user searching for a competitor's brand name is already problem-aware and in the final stages of the decision-making process. This is a prime opportunity. By bidding on their terms, we enter the conversation. We will prioritize competitors who are not aggressively defending their brand name with their own ads, as this presents a low-cost, high-reward opportunity.

**Action Plan:**

1. **Competitor Identification:** We will use Google Search, the "Auction Insights" report in Google Ads, and third-party tools to identify:
   * **Paid Competitors:** Companies actively bidding on our core service keywords.
   * **Organic Competitors:** Companies ranking in the top 10 for our core service keywords.
2. **Granular Campaign Structure:** We will create a dedicated "Competitor Campaign." Within this campaign, **each competitor will have its own ad group.**
   * Example: Ad Group 1: "Competitor A Keywords"; Ad Group 2: "Competitor B Keywords."
   * Benefit: This structure allows us to write highly tailored ad copy that mentions the specific competitor, increasing relevance and Click-Through Rate (CTR).
3. **Strategic Ad Copy & Landing Pages:**
   * **Ad Copy:** Our ads will be transparent and compelling. We will not impersonate the competitor. Instead, headlines will focus on comparison and value:
     + "Looking For [Competitor Name]?"
     + "A Better Alternative To [Competitor Name]"
     + "SMN vs. [Competitor]: See The Difference"
   * **Landing Pages:** We will create dedicated comparison landing pages. A generic homepage will not suffice. These pages will directly compare SMN's features, benefits, and pricing against the specific competitor, supported by client testimonials and a clear call-to-action (CTA). This is critical for maximizing conversion rates.

### **Phase 3: Asset Protection with a Brand Defense Campaign**

**Objective:** To secure your most valuable digital asset—your brand name—by preventing competitors from siphoning off traffic, controlling your message on the Search Engine Results Page (SERP), and achieving the highest possible conversion rate from your most qualified leads.

**Validity & Rationale:**This is the most crucial and cost-effective campaign we can run. Users searching directly for "Sales Manager Now" are at the bottom of the funnel and ready to engage. Allowing a competitor to show an ad above our organic listing is a preventable risk. Because of the perfect relevance between the keyword, ad, and landing page, this campaign will achieve a very high Quality Score (9-10/10), resulting in minimal CPCs and a dominant ad position.

**Action Plan:**

1. **Campaign & Keyword Setup:** We will create one dedicated "Brand" campaign targeting keywords such as:
   * sales manager now (Exact & Phrase)
   * salesmanagernow (Exact & Phrase)
   * Common misspellings of the brand name.
2. **Dominate the SERP with Ad Extensions:** Our goal is to take up as much real estate as possible. We will fully utilize every relevant ad extension, including:
   * **Sitelinks:** Direct links to key pages like "Our Services," "Case Studies," "Pricing," and "Contact Us."
   * **Callouts:** Short, impactful benefits like "Vetted Sales Managers," "No Long-Term Contracts," "Proven ROI."
   * **Structured Snippets:** Highlighting service categories (e.g., "Fractional Management," "Sales Coaching").
   * **Image Extensions:** Visually showcasing the professionalism of the SMN brand.
   * **Lead Form Extension:** Allowing users to convert directly from the SERP.
3. **Bidding & Budget:** We will set a small daily budget with a **Target Impression Share** bid strategy, aiming for **95%+ Absolute Top of Page** placement. This ensures we are always the #1 result for our own name.

# **Google Ads Strategic Growth Plan for Sales Manager Now (SMN)**

Our initial campaigns have given us a great starting point and valuable data. Now, it's time to evolve our strategy to capture a larger audience and drive more consistent growth.

We've developed a new three-step plan to unlock this next phase. We will:

* **Find more clients** by expanding beyond our current keywords.
* **Win business** from people looking at your competitors.
* **Protect your brand name** so you don't lose your most valuable leads.

This plan is designed to ignite lead flow and build a stronger, more reliable system for the future.

### **Phase 1: Reach Expansion & Discovery with Phrase Match**

**Objective:** Our goal is to find more customers by discovering all the different ways they search for services like yours. This will help us capture a larger audience and lower our overall cost per lead.

**Rationale:** Right now, we are targeting very specific phrases we know work. But people search for help in many different ways, and we're likely missing out on highly motivated buyers. This plan lets us "listen" for those valuable search variations. It’s a discovery tool that helps us find new, often less expensive, ways to attract the right people while learning more about what the market is looking for.

**The Pilot Proposal:**

* **Method:** We will launch a pilot campaign to discover the full range of how potential customers search for your services. This goes beyond the specific keywords we already know and helps us find new, profitable search terms.
* **Examples of New Opportunities:**
  + **Current Bid (Exact Match):** [fractional sales manager]
  + **What We're Missing:** This simple bid misses valuable, high-intent variations that real customers are searching for.
  + **What We'll Capture (Phrase Match):**
    - "fractional sales manager for small business"
    - "part time sales leader for tech startup"
    - "cost of a fractional sales manager"
    - "sales manager now vs hiring full time"
* **Pilot Cost & Timeline:** We propose a focused pilot budget of **$50 - $75 per day for 30 days**
  This is enough to gather meaningful data on new search queries and their profitability.
* **Confidence Level:** **High.** This is a standard, low-risk method for scaling successful Google Ads accounts. It is a proven way to find pockets of high-intent, lower-cost traffic.

### **Phase 2: Market Capture with Competitor Campaigns**

**Objective:** Our goal is to place SMN directly in front of potential clients at the exact moment they are looking up your competitors. This lets us capture serious buyers who we would have otherwise never reached.

**Rationale:** When someone searches for a competitor by name, they are a highly qualified lead. They have done their research and are close to making a decision. This is our chance to enter the conversation and show them why SMN is a better choice. It's a low-cost, high-reward strategy because we're targeting people who are already looking for a solution just like yours.

**The Pilot Proposal:**

* **Method:** We will run a test campaign to target users who are actively searching for your direct competitors. This allows us to enter the conversation at the final stage of their decision-making process and present SMN as a superior alternative.
* **Top Brands & Keywords to Test Against:** Based on our competitive analysis of companies targeting SMN’s keywords, we recommend starting a pilot targeting these key players:
  + **Competitor 1: Activated Scale**
    - Keywords: activated Scale sales manager, activated Scale alternative, activated Scale vs
  + **Competitor 2: TechCXO**
    - Keywords: techcxo fractional sales, techcxo sales manager
* **Pilot Cost & Timeline:** We propose a test budget of **$40 - $60 per day for 30 days**
* **Confidence Level:** **Medium to High.** The strategy is highly effective for lead generation. Our confidence is high that we will drive relevant clicks; the ultimate ROI will depend on how aggressively competitors bid to defend their own names, which our pilot is designed to measure.

### **Phase 3: Asset Protection with a Brand Defense Campaign**

**Objective:** Our goal is to own the top of Google for your own brand name. This prevents competitors from stealing your traffic, allows us to control the message, and converts your most valuable prospects at the highest possible rate.

**Rationale:** Someone searching for "Sales Manager Now" by name is your perfect customer—they already know you and are ready to talk. If we don't have an ad here, a competitor can easily place one above your free listing. This is the most affordable and important campaign we can run because Google rewards the perfect match of your ad with your brand name search, giving us the #1 spot for a very low cost.

**The Plan:**

* **Method:** We will launch an "always-on" campaign to protect your most valuable asset: your brand name. This ensures that when someone searches for "Sales Manager Now," we are the #1 result, preventing competitors from siphoning off your most qualified and ready-to-convert leads.
* **Estimated Cost:** **This is the most cost-effective campaign we will run.** The estimated cost is minimal, likely **$10 - $20 per day.** This is because the perfect relevance between the keyword ("Sales Manager Now"), our ad, and your website results in a near-perfect Quality Score (10/10), which Google rewards with extremely low ad costs.
* **Confidence Level:** **Very High.** This is an industry best practice with a guaranteed positive return. The risk of not doing this is far greater than the small cost to run it.
