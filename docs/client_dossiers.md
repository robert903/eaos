## 1. DEFINITE
**What the Client Does:** 
Definite is a modern, AI-powered Business Intelligence (BI) and data analytics platform. They position themselves as a direct, faster, and more intuitive replacement for legacy data stacks like Domo, Looker, Metabase, and Superset. Their platform allows users to query data, build dashboards, and automate reporting using AI, appealing to both technical data analysts and strategic business leaders.

**Target Audience (Personas):**
*   **Strategic Visionaries (Execs/Founders):** Need high-level dashboards and rapid answers without waiting on data teams.
*   **Operational Leaders (VP Ops/Sales):** Need reliable, real-time metrics to run their departments.
*   **Data Analysts:** Need a tool that removes boilerplate SQL writing so they can focus on complex modeling.
*   **IT Guardians:** Need secure, governed data access.

**What We Do For Them:**
We drive aggressive, competitor-targeted advertising and SEO. Our primary mandate is to capture search traffic from people looking for alternatives to Domo, Looker, etc.
*   **Paid Ads:** We manage Google Ads campaigns structured around "Alternative to [Competitor]" keywords. We constantly test ad copy, headlines, and descriptions to maximize conversions.
*   **SEO:** We map out category SEO strategies, identifying high-priority search terms and content gaps.
*   **Email Marketing:** We craft targeted outbound email sequences (segmented by the target company's employee count) that trigger based on website visitor signals, pushing Definite's value proposition against legacy stacks.

## 3. INNER BALANCE
**What the Client Does:**
Inner Balance is a specialized medical clinic focused on Hormone Replacement Therapy (HRT), menopause treatment, and medically supervised weight loss. They operate across the United States (with some specific regional exclusions/focus areas).

**Target Audience (Personas):**
Individuals (primarily women, but also men for specific therapies) experiencing symptoms of hormonal imbalance, such as fatigue, insomnia, weight gain, brain fog, and hot flashes.

**What We Do For Them:**
We execute a highly specialized Organic Growth and SEO strategy focused on patient acquisition. Because they are in the healthcare space (YMYL - Your Money or Your Life), content and technical SEO must be flawless.
*   **Technical SEO Remediation:** We conduct deep technical audits and implement fixes (resolving 404 errors, fixing canonical tags, restructuring heading hierarchies, and optimizing images).
*   **Content Strategy (Topic Modeling):** We build their content roadmap by mapping specific *Symptoms* (e.g., Fatigue) directly to *Conditions* (e.g., Menopause) and ultimately to the *Outcomes/Treatments* (HRT). We draft detailed briefs for articles (like "HRT 101") based on this matrix.
