# Standard Operating Procedure (SOP): Client Onboarding

Based on a comprehensive review of our active client engagements (360e, BRAND.AI, DATACOVES, DEFINITE, ENERGYBOT, INNER BALANCE, PET REGISTRATION AND RECOVERY, SALES MANAGER NOW), the following Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) has been developed to ensure a consistent, professional, and efficient onboarding experience for all new clients.

## Phase 1: Pre-Kickoff & Administration

**1. Contract & Agreement Finalization**
*   **Action:** Ensure all Independent Contractor Agreements or Freelance Contracts are signed by both parties.
*   **Key Elements:** Define the scope of work clearly (e.g., SEO audit vs. ongoing monthly management), explicit exclusions (e.g., "no implementation" for audits), compensation structure (hourly capped vs. retainer), and confidentiality clauses. *(Reference: ENERGYBOT, PET REGISTRATION)*

**2. Internal Resource Allocation**
*   **Action:** Assign the appropriate internal team members (e.g., Fractional Sales Manager, SEO Specialist, Ads Manager) based on the contracted services.

## Phase 2: System Access & Technical Setup

**1. Platform Access Request**
*   **Action:** Send a formal request to the client (or designated IT contact) for access to necessary platforms. Use a standardized checklist to ensure nothing is missed.
*   **Required Platforms (Service Dependent):**
    *   **Analytics:** Google Analytics (GA), Google Search Console (GSC), BigQuery (if applicable).
    *   **CMS/Website:** WordPress, Unbounce, or custom platform access.
    *   **Advertising:** Google Ads, Meta Ads, LinkedIn Ads.
    *   **Tracking:** Google Tag Manager.
    *   **CRM:** Attio, HubSpot, Salesforce, etc.

**2. Ad Accounts Setup & Linking**
*   **Action:** Link client ad accounts to our master Agency Manager Account.
*   **Verification:** Ensure appropriate billing is set up on the client side and that we have the necessary permission levels (Standard or Admin) to execute campaigns. *(Reference: BRAND.AI)*

## Phase 3: The Kickoff Meeting

**1. Objective & KPI Alignment**
*   **Action:** Conduct a formal kickoff meeting with key client stakeholders.
*   **Key Discussion Points:**
    *   Review the agreed-upon scope of work.
    *   Define primary Target KPIs (e.g., Cost Per Acquisition/Demo, target lead volume, ROAS). *(Reference: 360e)*

**2. Persona & Market Deep-Dive**
*   **Action:** Establish a clear understanding of the target audience and competitive landscape.
*   **Key Deliverables:**
    *   Define Ideal Customer Profiles (ICPs) / Personas (demographics, pain points, job titles). *(Reference: INNER BALANCE, DEFINITE)*
    *   Collect a list of primary competitors (URLs and key differentiators). *(Reference: ENERGYBOT)*
    *   Identify key product value propositions and "alternative to X" positioning strategies if applicable. *(Reference: DEFINITE)*

## Phase 4: Strategy & Roadmapping

**1. Initial Ecosystem Audit**
*   **Action:** Depending on the service level, conduct an initial audit of the existing ecosystem (e.g., Technical SEO audit, current Google Ads campaign structure analysis).

**2. Develop the Strategy Document**
*   **Action:** Create a documented strategy based on kickoff learnings and initial audits.
*   **Components:**
    *   **SEO & Content:** Topic modeling, keyword research, and a content roadmap. *(Reference: DATACOVES, INNER BALANCE)*
    *   **Paid Media:** Campaign structure proposals (e.g., competitor targeting vs. generic terms), budget allocation recommendations. *(Reference: SMN, DEFINITE)*

**3. Approval & Execution**
*   **Action:** Present the strategy document to the client for final approval before commencing execution or restructuring existing accounts.

## Phase 5: Ongoing Engagement & Reporting

**1. Monthly Check-Ins & Reporting**
*   **Action:** Establish a recurring meeting cadence (weekly or monthly) to review performance against the KPIs established in Phase 3.
*   **Key Discussion Points:** Review ad performance, organic traffic growth, experiment results (e.g., modifying ad copy or budgets), and prioritize upcoming to-dos. *(Reference: SMN, BRAND.AI coaching)*
